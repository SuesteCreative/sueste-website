# Sueste Marketing Strategy

This strategy document outlines our approach to growth and visibility, leveraging principles from the [marketingskills](https://github.com/coreyhaines31/marketingskills) repository.

## 1. Content Strategy (`content-strategy`)

Our content will focus on the intersection of **Design Elite** and **Technical Precision**.

### Core Pillars:
- **Case Studies**: In-depth breakdowns of our portfolio projects, focusing on the *impact* and *results* (e.g., "How we increased bookings for Alvor Watersports by 40%").
- **Design Process**: Behind-the-scenes looks at our creative workflow, drone shoots, and coding standards.
- **Client Education**: High-value content on why premium design matters for ROI.

## 2. Social Media Plan (`social-content`)

### Instagram & LinkedIn:
- **Visual Excellence**: Post only premium-grade visuals (high-res photos, drone 4K snippets).
- **Authority Posts**: Short, punchy videos or carousels explaining a single design or SEO tip.
- **Engagement**: Use polls and "This vs That" design comparisons to involve the community.

## 3. SEO & Discovery (`ai-seo` & `seo-audit`)

- **Programmatic SEO**: Create landing pages for specific niches (e.g., "Web Design for Architects in Algarve").
- **Schema Optimization**: Keep JSON-LD updated as we add more services or locations.
- **Speed as a Feature**: Maintain 95+ Lighthouse scores to ensure search engines favor our performant architecture.

## 4. Growth Engineering (`marketing-ideas`)

- **Free Tool Strategy**: Consider building a small, high-value free tool (e.g., "Website Speed Calculator for Creative Agencies") to capture leads.
- **Referral Program**: Reward existing clients who bring in new "Elite Tier" projects.
